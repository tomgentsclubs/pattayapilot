Frequently Asked Questions

Why are some crawls FREE?
We use our own "Pussy Wagons" to transport everyone around our group of Gents Clubs, so we offer these as a free perk. Monday and Friday crawls visit external venues requiring hired Baht Buses (350‡∏ø fee).
How much will I spend?
If you just have one drink at each venue, expect to spend less than 1,000 baht for the entire crawl. Want to buy lady drinks and interact more? Budget around 2,500-5,000 baht. The sky's the limit if you want to party hard!
Can I join late or leave early?
Yes! Check the route times and meet us at any venue. You can also leave whenever you want, though you'll miss the beer card raffle if you leave before the final stop!
Is there time for "short-time"?
We spend 60 minutes per venue, so if you can arrange it quickly, yes! Many people stay longer and catch up via taxi to the next stop.
Will there be food?
Yes! All FREE Pussy Wagon crawls include complimentary food: Tuesday (Free Goulash at Free Willy), Wednesday (Free Lasagne at Camel Toe), Thursday (Free Lasagne at Camel Toe), Saturday (Free Chicken Wings & Dips at CatFlaps).
What's the beer card raffle?
On every FREE Pussy Wagon crawl, we hand out raffle tickets at the start. Stay until the end and you could win a beer card loaded with 10 FREE beers, valid at all our Gents Clubs with no expiry date! The raffle is drawn at the final venue around 8:30 PM.
Do I need to book in advance?
FREE Pussy Wagon crawls require no booking - just show up at the meet point at the specified time. Buzzin crawls (Monday & Friday) require booking through their Discord server. Buzzin Discord
What's "THE DARKSIDE"?
THE DARKSIDE is East Pattaya's mysterious nightlife zone, home to Maggie Mays Darkside. Wednesday and Saturday crawls venture into this area for a different vibe and experience. It's perfect for those who want to explore beyond the usual tourist spots!
